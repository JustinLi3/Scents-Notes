How to Buy Fragrances

Buying a fragrance can be a great experience, but it’s important to do it the right way to avoid fake or expired products. Here’s my personal method for buying fragrances online:

1. Choose Your Platform

I recommend using platforms like eBay or Mercari. These platforms often have great deals, but you need to be cautious.

2. Check the Batch Number

Before making a purchase, always check or ask the seller for the batch number. This number is usually printed on the box or the bottom of the bottle. Once you have it:

  1. Go to CheckCosmetic.net
  2. Enter the batch number to verify the manufacturing date and ensure the product isn’t expired.

3. Inspect the Pictures

Carefully review the pictures provided by the seller. Look for:

  1. Clear, high-quality images of the bottle and box.
  2. Details such as the font, logo, and packaging that match the original product.

4. Check Reviews

Always check the seller’s reviews and ratings:

  1. Look for sellers with high ratings and positive feedback.
  2. Read through recent reviews to ensure their products are authentic.

5. Trust Your Instincts

If something feels off—whether it’s the price, the photos, or the seller’s communication—trust your instincts and move on to another listing.

Conclusion

Following these steps can help you get great deals on cologne while avoiding fakes and expired products. Happy shopping!
